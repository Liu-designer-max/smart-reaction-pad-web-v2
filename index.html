Measurement Notes

  • Response time: target light to first FSR contact. It is not pure nerve reaction time.
  • FSR signal: ADC values show relative sensor level, not force, body weight, or ground reaction force.
  • Side comparison: left and right mean target position. The pad does not automatically know which leg was used.
  • Go/No-Go: red trials test stepping response. Green trials test the ability to hold back a step.
  • Use: this is an educational prototype for RTP discussion, not a clinical clearance tool.
